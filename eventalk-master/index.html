About

About the Event

IUCEE Annual Student Forum is an opportunity for engineering students from different parts of the country to assemble and apply engineering and technology to develop solutions to real world challenges in an intense 3 day long conference.

IASF PROGRAMS

Transitioning into Engineering and Graduating Out

Interdisciplinary Discovery Bootcamp

Wisdom is a continuing assimilation of experiences. Interactive, collaborative learning enhances these experiences into lifelong impressions.Interdisciplinary Discovery bootcamp is designed on the principles of STEM learning to expose the fresh UGs to real world applications of scientific principles.

Interdisciplinary Design Thinking Bootcamp

Thinking is a skill. Solution finding and problem identification is one of the most essential skills for engineers. This bootcamp helps students to channelize their thinking for value creation and innovation. Bootcamp will be highly interactive using role play, story telling and biomimicry.

Engineering Excellence Skills Workshop

Practical skills and expert guidance from industry workshops provide final year students with a leading edge for a confident transition into their engineering careers. These carefully crafted sessions, led by professionals, aim to empower students for excellence in the professional world.

Preparation for IASF

Hit the ground running, so you can hit the sky flying.

We want our IASF participants to derive maximum benefits from the live bootcamp in the presence of coaches.

Believe it or not your experiential learning will be enhanced if you attend the series of online webinars starting from 30th September. Click here to check the online webinar calendar.

The ideas initiated during IASF will develop under the guidance from mentors from January to March 2024 in online mode.

The bootcamps will continue in online mode beyond IASF dates. The bootcamps will conclude with a GRAND FINALE online in March 2024.

STUDENT CHAPTER PROJECT EXHIBITION

Opportunity to showcase their best projects to inspire their fraternity and, receive guidance and mentoring from experts
  • 20 Project Posters will be selected and invited to exhibit at IASF 2024
  • Announcement of Projects selected by IUCEE to be exhibited at IASF 2024: 15, December 2023.
  • Projects will be invited based on their understanding of the problem backed by data, the impact of the solution to society, optimal usage of resources, project planning and quality of report.
  • The top 3 Student Projects will be declared as winners at IASF 2024 and will be awarded with a token prize money.
